# Brand Validation: Mixelith

Mixelith is the chosen name for the project, product, and repository.

## Current Decision

- Product Name: **Mixelith**.
- Flutter Project Name: `mixelith`.
- Android Package: `com.mixelith`.
- GitHub Repository: `https://github.com/massimomazzariol/Mixelith.git`.

## Meaning

Mixelith combines:

- **mix**, evoking transformation, color blending, and pixel processing;
- **lith**, referring to lithography, stone, material, artistic print, and texture.

The name supports a minimal, premium, and artistic positioning, closer to a serious graphic tool than a generic commercial photo editor.

## Validations to Complete

Before commercial publication, manual checks and, if the project is distributed publicly, professional legal verifications remain necessary.

- Google Play Store: Identical or confusingly similar names.
- Apple App Store: Identical or confusingly similar names.
- GitHub: Repository or libraries with the same name.
- Domains: Availability of `.app`, `.io`, `.net`, or alternatives.
- Social Handles: Availability of major public handles.
- TMview/EUIPO: Preliminary trademark search for software and digital service classes.
- Legal Consultation: Professional search for prior rights if requested for commercial release.

## Legal Note

This document does not constitute legal advice. The internal choice of the name does not replace professional checks on trademarks, app stores, domains, or social handles.
